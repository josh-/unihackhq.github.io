How do I get there?

The best way to get to Gurrowa Lab is to catch a train to Parliament station, and take the Lonsdale Street Exit. Alternatively, take a train to Melbourne Central and walk down Lonsdale Street.

The venue is also located adjacent to the Elizabeth Street bus interchange, accessible to bus routes 302 303, 304, 305, 309, 318, 350, 905, 906, 907 and 908.
