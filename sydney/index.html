How do I get there?

The best way to get to Stone and Chalk is taking a train/ferry to Circular Quay, then walking down to the corner of Young Street and Bridge Street. For those who wish to take a bus, the nearest bus stop is Circular Quay, Phillip St, Stand C.

We suggest you visit Transport NSW's website at transportnsw.info to plan your travel for the weekend.

Diversity

Unfortunately, there are some groups that are underrepresented in the IT community. These groups include (but are not limited to) women, people of colour, the LGBTQIA+ community, people with disabilities, and those from economically or socially disadvantaged backgrounds who would be unable to attend without some financial assistance.

We value diversity and inclusion at UNIHACK Sydney, and believe expressing and offering support is the first step to progress. As such, we like to invite competitors from these underrepresented backgrounds to apply for the Diversity Scholarship, so that we can support you with a discounted entry!
