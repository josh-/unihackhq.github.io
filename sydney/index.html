How do I get there?

The best way to get to Stone and Chalk is taking a train/ferry to Circular Quay, then walking down to the corner of Young Street and Bridge Street. For those who wish to take a bus, the nearest bus stop is Circular Quay, Phillip St, Stand C.

We suggest you visit Transport NSW's website at transportnsw.info to plan your travel for the weekend.
